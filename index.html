WELCOME TO JUDWORD GUESSING GAME

Let's guess the Word

_ _ _ _ _ _

Instructions

  1. The game is played by turns: Player 1, then Player 2
  2. When it's a player's turn, he has 30 seconds to guess the word
  3. Each word winned equals 1 point, thus score increases by 1
  4. The player to reach 10 points wins the game
